    Backgrounds (Campaign-Specific)¶

    Pathfinder 2e's standard backgrounds all work in Aetherion. The list below adds campaign-specific options — picks that give your character a deeper foothold in the world, a tie to a faction, or a story-hook the GM already knows how to use.

    Each grants the standard PF2e background package: a free ability boost plus a choice between two listed boosts, training in one skill, training in one lore skill, and a granted skill feat.

    A few backgrounds are tagged "Talk to the GM" — these have heretical or politically sensitive ties in the setting. The tag isn't a "no"; it's a "drop me a line first so the campaign opening can handle your situation gracefully." Approvals are usually quick.

    Don't see your concept? Reach out to the GM. If you have a character idea that isn't on the list — a different profession, a different upbringing, a hook this slate doesn't capture — we can build a custom background for it together. Custom backgrounds get the same standard PF2e package (boost choice + free boost, trained skill, trained lore, granted skill feat); we just pick the four pieces that fit your concept. Bring me a sentence or two about who your character was before the campaign and we'll work backwards from there.

    Mechanical references (skills, feats) below link to the Archives of Nethys for quick reference. Lore skills are campaign-specific to Aetherion and don't have AoN entries.

    Patron-side (Yverin's circle)¶

    Shop Apprentice¶

    You apprenticed to Yverin at his antique shop in Aurion's mid-quarter for a year or more. You know where things are kept, which customers to be wary of, and which days the deliveries come.

    Boost: Int or Cha · Skill: Society · Lore: Aurion · Feat: Streetwise

    Yverin's Courier¶

    You run the Antiquarian's deliveries across Aurion and occasionally farther. You know the back routes, the discreet drop-houses, and which Skyguard patrols move when.

    Boost: Dex or Wis · Skill: Stealth · Lore: Aurion · Feat: Pickpocket

    The Antiquarian's Youngest¶

    You're the youngest of Yverin's hires, and he's taken a personal interest. He makes you tea. He tells you old stories. You don't know why you, but you don't mind.

    Boost: Int or Cha · Skill: Society · Lore: Antiquarian · Feat: Bon Mot

    The Shop Translator¶

    Yverin's correspondence runs through more languages than is reasonable. You handle the old-tongue paperwork — archaic dwarven, long-dead scripts, dialects no one speaks aloud anymore.

    Boost: Int or Wis · Skill: Society · Lore: Old-Tongue · Feat: Multilingual

    Highcrown and institutions¶

    Inquisition Castaway (Talk to the GM)¶

    You were a sworn Skyward Inquisitor or initiate, and you left. Whether they let you go or you ran, they know your name and your record.

    Boost: Str or Wis · Skill: Religion · Lore: Inquisition · Feat: Recognize Spell

    Crown Assembly Page¶

    You served a noble House of the Crown Assembly — kept their schedules, carried their letters, learned which fork was for which course. You know how Highcrown politics actually works.

    Boost: Cha or Int · Skill: Diplomacy · Lore: Heraldry · Feat: Courtly Graces

    Disgraced Heir¶

    You were once a minor House heir. A feud, a scandal, a wrong cousin — your name was taken. The manners and the connections remain.

    Boost: Cha or Int · Skill: Deception · Lore: Genealogy · Feat: Lie to Me

    Bank of Aurion Clerk¶

    You kept ledgers in one of Aurion's great mercantile houses. The accounts told you whose money came from where; the things rich families try to hide eventually pass through a clerk's desk.

    Boost: Int or Wis · Skill: Society · Lore: Mercantile · Feat: Quick Identification

    Skyguard Veteran¶

    You served honorably in the Skyguard. Whether you mustered out, were invalided, or just walked away — the discipline stuck, and so did the wounds.

    Boost: Str or Con · Skill: Athletics · Lore: Warfare · Feat: Hefty Hauler

    Cloud Knight Squire¶

    You almost made knighthood with the Cloud Knights of Aurion. Almost. You can wear the gear; you can quote the codes; the spurs were never struck.

    Boost: Str or Cha · Skill: Diplomacy · Lore: Heraldry · Feat: Group Impression

    Sylvaris and scholarship¶

    Sylvari Archivist's Aide¶

    You apprenticed at the Archivists of Moonbough — the Great Library beneath the canopy city. You can read in the dim; you can find a misshelved scroll by smell.

    Boost: Int or Wis · Skill: Society · Lore: Archive · Feat: Assurance (Society)

    Aetheric Collegium Initiate¶

    You trained as a sanctioned shard-scholar at the Collegium. You've handled relics under proper authority and passed your initiate's tests. The Collegium watches what you do with the credential.

    Boost: Int or Wis · Skill: Arcana · Lore: Shard · Feat: Recognize Spell

    Lanternbearer's Apprentice (Talk to the GM)¶

    Your teacher was a Lanternbearer — one of the outlawed shard-mystics whose practice the Inquisition does not recognize. They taught you anyway. You hope your name isn't on a list.

    Boost: Wis or Cha · Skill: Occultism · Lore: Shard · Feat: Trick Magic Item

    Failed Apprentice¶

    You washed out of an academy — Crowngear's, the Sylvari conservatories, a noble house's private tutoring program. You know the systems from the inside. The systems don't miss you.

    Boost: Int (plus free) · Skill: Crafting or Society · Lore: pick one (Crowngear / Sylvari / Aurion / etc.) · Feat: Quick Identification

    Ankarum and dwarven craft¶

    Khalvar Forge-Soldier¶

    You trained in Khalvar Hold's tradition of warrior-craftsmen — soldiers who keep their own gear, smiths who can hold a line. The forge is a battlefield; the battlefield is a forge.

    Boost: Str or Con · Skill: Crafting · Lore: Warfare · Feat: Specialty Crafting

    Ember Faith Pilgrim (Talk to the GM)¶

    You walked the mourning routes of the Ember Faith — the dwarven splinter sect that keeps vigil for the lost Red Ember. The Court tolerates them as fringe. Tolerance, you've learned, is a thin thing.

    Boost: Wis or Con · Skill: Religion · Lore: Ember Faith · Feat: Student of the Canon

    Black Anvil Initiate (Talk to the GM)¶

    You ran with the Black Anvil — Ankarum's heretic cult that searches the mountain interior for "ancient shard-forges." Whether you came up with anything, you know the deep tunnels.

    Boost: Con or Int · Skill: Crafting · Lore: Underground · Feat: Specialty Crafting (Blacksmithing)

    Keelwright Mason¶

    You apprenticed with the Keelwrights of Ankarum — the engineering guild that quite literally holds Aetherion together. Stone, rune, and load-bearing arches are your trade.

    Boost: Str or Int · Skill: Crafting · Lore: Engineering · Feat: Specialty Crafting (Stonemasonry)

    Glymswick and engineering¶

    Crowngear Engineer¶

    You trained at Cogbarrow under the Gearwright's Consortium. Drift control, turbines, the calibrated madness that keeps Aetherion in the air — you can read the schematics and fix the parts.

    Boost: Int or Dex · Skill: Crafting · Lore: Crowngear · Feat: Specialty Crafting

    Mausoleum Attendant¶

    You worked the Last Gate Mausoleum in Cogbarrow — gnomish funerary craft, clockwork ossuaries, the precision of mourning. The dead get the dignity of the very best engineering.

    Boost: Wis or Con · Skill: Religion · Lore: Mortuary · Feat: Student of the Canon

    Tumblebrook and the road¶

    Caravan Outrider¶

    You rode with the League of Feathered Roads — the halfling caravan-guild whose routes web the continent. Every skybridge worth crossing, you've crossed.

    Boost: Dex or Con · Skill: Survival · Lore: Sky Travel · Feat: Forager

    Hedge Witch's Apprentice¶

    You learned from a Tumblebrook hedge witch — the unofficial folk-magic of the countryside, the cures and the curses and the way to read a sky for rain. Quiet work, mostly tolerated.

    Boost: Wis or Cha · Skill: Nature or Occultism · Lore: Herbalism · Feat: Natural Medicine

    Festival Officiate¶

    You're Mirth's lay clergy — the people who actually organize the festivals while the gods get the credit. Lights, music, food, fire-safety, drunken pilgrims, gentle drunks at dawn. You handle it.

    Boost: Cha or Con · Skill: Performance · Lore: Festival · Feat: Fascinating Performance

    Tether's End and the liminal¶

    Tether's End Native¶

    You grew up in the descent town — the only place on the continent that touches the world below. You've seen lifts move things no one talks about. You've seen smugglers stop talking when you walked in.

    Boost: Dex or Wis · Skill: Survival · Lore: Tether's End · Feat: Streetwise

    Salvage Hauler¶

    You worked the lift gangs at the Grand Anchor — pulling wreckage, crates, the occasional thing that should not have come up. The Salvage Syndicate took its cut. You took yours quietly.

    Boost: Str or Wis · Skill: Athletics · Lore: Salvage · Feat: Hefty Hauler

    Religious vocations¶

    Hospice Volunteer¶

    You worked at one of Mercy's hospices — the largest is Aurelium Hospice in Aurion, but the network runs everywhere. Bedside hours, long ones. You know death and you know what to say to the dying.

    Boost: Wis or Cha · Skill: Medicine · Lore: Mercy · Feat: Battle Medicine

    Cloistered Devotee¶

    You were raised in a temple to one of the Twelve. You knew the daily rites before you knew the alphabet. You have, possibly, lapsed. The training stuck.

    Boost: Wis or Cha · Skill: Religion · Lore: pick a specific god · Feat: Student of the Canon

    Mortis's Witness¶

    You were present at a death of consequence — a soldier in honest cause, a healer mid-rescue, a maker finishing one last piece. Mortis came. You remember. Some people say the look in your eyes hasn't quite settled since.

    Boost: Wis or Con · Skill: Religion · Lore: Mortis · Feat: Diehard

    Outsider and wanderer¶

    Skyfall Survivor¶

    You were caught in a Skyfall event as a child and lived. The experience left you... attuned to certain stones. You can feel them before you see them.

    Boost: Con or Wis · Skill: Occultism · Lore: Skyfall · Feat: Shard Sense (campaign-specific feat, coming)

    Streetwise Orphan¶

    You grew up in Aurion's underbelly — the alleys, the rooftops, the kitchens where the back door is always unlocked. You know every alley because the alleys were home.

    Boost: Dex or Cha · Skill: Stealth · Lore: Aurion · Feat: Pickpocket

    Awakened animal¶

    These give an awakened animal a place in the world. Talk to the GM about which fits your concept; we'll work out the rules path together.

    Caged Wonder¶

    You were an awakened animal in an oddities-collector's menagerie — a curiosity, a conversation piece, a thing the rich pointed at over wine. You're out now.

    Boost: Con or Cha · Skill: Acrobatics · Lore: Aurion · Feat: Cat Fall

    Hospice Companion¶

    You were an awakened animal kept by Mercy's hospices for therapeutic work — bedside visits to the dying, gentle presence in long nights. They let you choose your own path when you asked.

    Boost: Wis or Con · Skill: Medicine · Lore: Mercy · Feat: Natural Medicine

    What if I don't see what I want?¶

    Talk to the GM. Backgrounds are the easiest thing in the system to bend or build new from scratch — if you have a specific concept that doesn't fit any of the above, we'll figure it out together.
